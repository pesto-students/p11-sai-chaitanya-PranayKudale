My Hometown: Nashik

Nashik, Maharashtra

Location

Nashik is located 165 kms from state capital Mumbai.

About

Nashik is a city in the state of Maharashtra, located in western India. It is situated on the banks of the Godavari river, and is known for its historical and cultural significance. Nashik is also known as the "Wine Capital of India" due to its thriving wine industry.

Weather

The city's tropical location and high altitude combine to give it a relatively mild version of a hot semi-arid climate.

Population

Nashik has a population around 2,962,769.

Things To Do In Nashik

  • Visit Pandavleni Caves
  • Visit Sula Vineyards
  • Visit Trimbakeshwar Temple
  • Visit Kalaram Temple
  • Visit Someshwar Waterfall
